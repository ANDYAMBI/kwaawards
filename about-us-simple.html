About Us

Karnataka Women Achievers' Awards 2018 (KWAA)
While it’s not easy to be a woman. It’s definitely never so being a woman achiever!!

Right from the start of life, a woman is handed down multiple roles that she so beautifully multi tasks! There is no limit to what we women can accomplish. The Empowered woman is powerful beyond measure and beautiful beyond description focused, intelligent, motivated, strong, dignified, she works with passion towards her set goals, she defines success on her own terms, achieves it by her own rules and reaches heights she is proud of

Are you her? If you think you fit the description of a “successful woman” having carved a unique distinctive mark in society with your work, then send in your entries. Winners will be selected by a credited jury. The awards will be bestowed at a star studded red carpet event in a prominent venue. A fashion show by a celebrity designer will add glitz and glamour to this glorious evening

Who can nominate?

Anyone who is 18 years and above can nominate themselves in any of the 18 categories they feel that they fit in or they can also nominate women achievers they know personally (could be your mother, sister, mother-in-law, cousins, colleagues or friends) You can also nominate same person for different categories, but multiple nominations for a person in the same category will be disqualified

OUR GOALS

Our quest through this award ceremony is to recognise and honour every woman irrespective of age, class, caste or religion. Our pursuit is to applaud her unique contribution in making a change in society through her chosen field, the one who has paved the way holds the torch for future generations to follow

SPOORTHI VISHWAS FOUNDER   KWAA - KARNATAKA WOMEN ACHIEVERS' AWARDS
FOUNDER'S MESSAGE

“You are unique, there has never been another you and there will never be. so live your best life”

As a much-loved daughter I was raised to think independently and make smart choices. Proud of being a South Indian actress who performed in three languages, a double graduate. Managing Director of Event Art that made news for the trend setting “Pantaloons Face Of Bangalore”. Yet marriage and giving birth to a daughter changed life. In the Indian Cultura a woman is expected to fit into stereotypical roles and somewhere along the way you tend to feel stifled and confined and that stunts the growth of your personal goals and dreams. The life of a home maker is harder and more challenging than any job on earth!! These thoughts sowed the seeds for KWAA – Karnataka Women Achiever Awards!!

Because I believe each time a woman stands up for herself, she stands up for all women!! Women are the largest untapped reservoir of talent in the world. By recognising and awarding such immense talent and strength from all walks, categories and cadre of society these awards will empower more women to free their creative self and blossom. We can show the world that the woman draws her strength from within and reaches glorious heights if she makes up her powerful mind. Being empowered she can handle the role of Home maker and career woman very effectively!!
My motto in life has always been..to be unique and different!! So BE SPECIAL..BE YOU!!
