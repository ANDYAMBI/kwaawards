KWAA RECOGNITION AWARD CATEGORIES

01.

Unique
Woman Award

Woman who have dared to be different have done something unthinkable and blazed the trail for the future generations

02.

Role
Model Award

Woman who has chosen a positive attitude towards challenging situations and sets an example for others to follow. Her values, attitude and behaviour associated with her work distinguish her in such a way other admire and emulate her

03.

Woman in Media
award

Woman with exceptional endeavours in television, radio, newspaper and publication. She uses her prowess in her extraordinary fearless style of reporting and presenting

Young woman who has motivated and revolutionized the society through her social work in schools, colleges or community thereby bringing about a change

Woman who have exhibited meritus acts of bravery against all odds,an act or deed in the face of risk to life or threat of physical injury or an act of courage and fearlessness against a social evil or crime

Woman who has made a mark in the fashion industry through her spectacular work in making others look good or models who has graced the ramp!

08.

Entrepreneur

Woman who has started her own business, an idea that she puts into an initiative beyond excellence, providing and creating jobs and revenue for others. With her unique vision she fosters economic empowerment for herself and society at large

Academia

Woman who has excelled in the academic field and hold esteemed positions in any academic division

Entertainer

Senior citizens still actively contributing to family and society. Highly experienced and wise they made a tremendous impact with their valuable work

Physically challenged yet a strong will power and mind to do the extraordinary. The physical impediment not a hindrance in achieving her dreams. She is an inspiration to many

Single and strong, financially independent, she balances home and work with a flair! While making sure her loved ones miss nothing

Woman who has raised her children having faced many challenging and difficult situations. She loves unconditionally placing the needs of her children at the forefront, offering herself completely
